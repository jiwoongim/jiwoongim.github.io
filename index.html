About Daniel

I am a CEO and Founder of Ubet. In the past, I was a CEO of AIFounded.inc, an a CTO of Coinscious.inc.

Previously, I was a research scientist at Janelia Research Centre, Branson Lab under the advisory of Kristin Branson. Also, I was a researcher at the University of Montreal, MILA machine learning institute under the advisory of Yoshua Bengio and Roland Memisevic. I received my Masters degree from the University of Guelph's School of Engineering department under the advisory of Graham W. Taylor. I received my undergraduate degree from the University of Toronto in 2012. I completed a specialist program in Computer Science with a specialization in Artifical Intelligence Specialist and pursued Mathematics and It's Application specialist program.

My primary interests in working with deep learning models and analyzing unsupervised representatation learning models under the view of dynamical system or probabilistic perspective.
